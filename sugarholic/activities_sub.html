ACTIVITIES

  • Make your own Cookie (DIY)

    Bring your friends and kids to the DIY Cookie session. You can cookie anything! We've had tangerine cookies, onion cookies, avocado cookies, and even kimchi cookies! Be the next to create the most unique one and only cookie.

    Everyday 10AM - 4PM

    Location: The Butter Cookies Hive

  • The Cookie Batter Fight

    The Cookie Batter Fight is just like a mudfight but cookie batter instead of mud! Everything will be edible from the batter and sprinkles on the fight. Have a blast with your family and friends!

    Only Saturdays 3PM - 6PM

    Location: The Cookie Plaza

  • Gummy Bear Hunt

    We dare you to explore the mysterious woods to find the Golden Gummy Bear. You never know what you will encounter in the mysterious woods so get ready for an adventure. A prize of $10,000 will be given to the winner returning with the Golden Gummy Bear!

    Fridays & Saturdays 6PM - 10PM

    ** Children younger than the age of 12 must be assisted by an adult.

    Location: The Mysterious Woods

  • Rock Candy Bowling

    Bring your group to the Rock Candy Bowling alley where all our bowling balls are made out of huge rock candies. Wipe out the buscuit pins and set a new record!

    Everyday 11AM - 9PM

    Location: The Rock Candy Bowling Alley
