ROOMS

The Pool Deluxe

Enjoy the special view of our beautiful private garden in front of the rocky-road chocolate mountain. The Pool Deluxe provides a private pink lemonade swimming pool upon request. Request must be made at least 24 hours in advance.

Max number of guests: 4.

The Pool Premier

Our Pool premier provides a more spacious room suitable for larger groups. Enjoy the ocean view from your balcony where we have the indoor pool and jacuzzi. The Pool Premier provides a private pink lemonade swimming pool & herbal tea jacuzzi upon request. Request must be made at least 24 hours in advance.

Max number of guests: 6.

The Pool Suite

The Pool Suite is best for guests of large groups up to 8 people. Feel free to cook your own meal in our luxury in suite kitchen. Enjoy the ocean view from right upfront. The Pool Premier provides a private pink lemonade swimming pool & herbal tea jacuzzi upon request. Request must be made at least 24 hours in advance.

Max number of guests: 8.

Beach Bungalow

The Bungalow is a private house located at our sandy breadcrumb beach. Enjoy the ocean right in front of you! Private beach barbecue parties available on request 24 hours in advance.

Only available during summer season.
Max number of guests: 6.
