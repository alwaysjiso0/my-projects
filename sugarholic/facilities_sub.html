FACILITIES

  • Pink Lemonade Pool

    Sugarholic Resort&Spa signature pink lemonade pool. Freshly made pink lemonade is filled in our main ourdoor swimming pool everyday.

    Open: Everyday 8AM - 8PM

  • The Chocolate Creek

    At the step of our mysterious woods, we have our one and only chocolate creek flowing with thick dark chocolate.

    Enquire at the Information Center for entry.

  • The Hot Cocoa Bath

    With the fresh thick dark chocolate provided from the Chocolate Creek in our woods, we made a Hot Cocoa Bath just for our visitors. The chocolate from the creek is special, great to make glowing smooth skin.

    Open: Monday to Saturday 10AM - 8PM

  • Scentaholic Spa

    The Scentaholic Spa uses all-natural herbal tea and oil to give you the most relaxing treatment. Enlighten your senses for an ultimate heal of your body.

    Open: Everyday 11AM - 11PM

  • The Ice cream Slope

    Bring your friends and family to the Ice cream Slope, made of 100% pure vanilla icecream. Why not have a taste while your on your sled?

    Open: Everyday 11AM - 7PM

  • Blue Lemonade Valley

    Take a trip to our hidden Blue Lemonade Valley, only accessible with the guide of our Sugarholic staff. Have a ride in a transparent canoe on the crystal clear blue lemonade lake.

    Available: Monday to Friday 6AM - 3PM
