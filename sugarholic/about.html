"The Sweetest Paradise for Everyone"

We aim to be the most relaxing yet exciting sanctuary for all our visitors.
The Sugarholic Resort&Spa is the first resort to offer an everything-sweets themed resort.
Our 20,000 square meters of land has eye-opening attractions and facilities.

Escape from hectic days of work and have a fun and relaxing time at our Sugarholic Resort&Spa!

Room

We offer all-private rooms for our customers. All architectures of the Sugarholic Resoprt&Spa is an artwork made by the famous architect Jennifer Choi. You can't miss the overwhelming view of our beautiful island from your room.

Recreation

Enjoy a wide range of various activities in our unique facilities and natural sites only available at the Sugarholic island. Take pictures at spots where no one else can take unless they visit the island. The sweetest activities are prepared for your relaxing yet exciting stay at our island!

Food

We have a total of 18 diners in our island, yet we are most proud of our signature fine dining restaurant "The fine Diners" and ofcourse our own desserts brand "The Sugarholic Sweets & Bakery". Try our gourmet food and desserts only available at the Sugarholic island.
