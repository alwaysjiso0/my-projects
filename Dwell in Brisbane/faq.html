Frequently Asked Questions

  • What should I do if I come across a house whose location you are satisfied with and want to mark?

    You can mark the map directly by left-clicking on it.

  • What do you do if you do not want to fill in the relevant information?

    You can skip this page by clicking on the skip in the bottom right corner to go to the next page.

  • What does the favorites list do?

    It will gather and display all your selected favorite areas on the map page.

  • What will display on the recommendations page?

    On the recommendations page, our website will give you the top three areas(places) based on your preference that selected on the "Tell us" page.
