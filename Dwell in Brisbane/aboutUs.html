Our Story

We are a group of individuals from different corners of the world with a common goal to study in Brisbane. Coming from different places and with different backgrounds, we wanted to search for a dwelling that suits our specific needs. For example, One of us wanted to live near the university with good transportation facilities to all the parts of the city, whereas one among us wanted to live in a place that has good schools and kids parks. Although there were several real estate searching apps, we couldnt find something that could suggest a neighbourhood that suits our needs. Hence we decided to get our hands on and buid this app which would show the best suited localities for specific needs rather than a house search on real estate portal. This is our humble attempt to come up with the solution for a problem we faced and we are sure everyone who moves in to brisbane has this problem.

Our Goal

Simplify and provide convenience to prospective residents of Brisbane
